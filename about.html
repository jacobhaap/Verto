What is Verto?

Verto is a network for startup companies to connect and interact with other startup companies. From here they can exchange services and ideas to help each other out. For example, an IT company and a construction company can make an exchange where the construction company will do some work on their office in exchange of the IT company creating/fixing up their website.

What do we do?

What we do is act as the middle-man. We put the system in place for the companies to communicate with each other. THey simply log on to our website, then can make a post or search through recent postings and make their connections.

Verto Token

The Verto Token is a token deployed on the Ethereum blockchain. Each token is used as a contract between two startup companies when there is an exchange of ideas or services. When two startup companies would like to make a connection, one Verto Token transaction goes through on the Ethereum blockchain to create their contract. Verto tokens can be stored in any ERC-20 Ethereum wallet, making them very easy for users to store and use.
